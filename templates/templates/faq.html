What's the best way to save on packing and moving services?

As you already know, the movers and packers services are charges as per specific needs of an individual. There are many factors that are considered while estimating the cost of moving or storage, size of move and distance being the two major factors. If you want to cut the moving or storage costs, then look at the inventory and double check if there is any item that you don't really need to move. Apart from that, always check quotations and rates from at least two or three moving companies to get fair idea of the costs involved in moving and storage of your inventory. And third, do negotiate with the moving companies, as they are open to negotiation to some extent like any other service providing company.
